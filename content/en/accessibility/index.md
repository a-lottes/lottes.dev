I run this website as an individual creator and continuously work to make its content as accessible as possible.

## Compliance status

This website is currently **partially compliant** with accessibility requirements.

This assessment is based on an editorial self-review of key pages and on baseline technical checks in current browsers.

## Non-accessible content

Despite ongoing improvements, the following barriers may still occur:

- Some contrast ratios in components with alternative background colors may be too low.
- Not all linked documents or externally embedded media are available in fully accessible alternative formats.
- Some link texts or headings in older content may not always be sufficiently descriptive.
- Certain dynamic content may not be announced consistently across all browser and screen-reader combinations.

## Ongoing improvements

The following actions are currently prioritized:

- Systematically review and standardize contrast and focus states.
- Improve descriptive quality of link texts and headings in legacy content.
- Add or refine alternative text for relevant images and graphics.
- Regularly test keyboard navigation and screen-reader output in recurring components.
