This website does not collect personally identifiable data. The audience measurement tool used on this site is configured so that collected information is anonymized.

Cookies stored on your device help me understand visit volume and the most viewed pages, without directly identifying you.

This setup follows the recommendations of the [CNIL](https://www.cnil.fr/fr/solutions-pour-les-cookies-de-mesure-daudience).
